A quick guide to your low-sodium journey

Simple Swaps for Healthy Hearts!

OH NO! You have just been told by your doctor that you have high blood pressure, diabetes or a heart condition. Or maybe you just want to be little bit healthier and stay ahead of mother nature.

In a world where we are bombarded with unsolicited health advice, trendy cleanses, and miracle weight loss products, how can we separate fact from fiction.

There are many important changes you can make to have a healthy heart, but focusing on heart health for many starts at the grocery store.

Don't think that you have to make big changes to have an effect on your heart health, though. Even small, basic steps can have dramatic effects.

Simple Start Popular Swaps

We wanted to provide a resource where consumers can see some easy swaps you can make at the grocery store and in your cooking that will instantly lower your sodium intake and increase the health of your heart.

It is not hard to find great products to replace your overly salty standards. Once you make these changes at home your taste buds will thank and in 21 days you will break your salt addiction.

Food will actually taste better and you will appreciate other flavor profiles you may have never explored. So don’t look at these changes as missing out but as focusing on your long term health and well being.

About Me

My Name is Jesse Daniels

In September of 2019 I noticed common chores were causing me to be out of breath. In October, I realized that this was not normal out of shape behavior. I was only 38 years old when a doctor at an urgent care facility said I needed to go to the hospital. I spent a week in the cardiac unit of Norton’s. My blood pressure was elevated and I had accumulated fluid around my heart. I was diagnosed with congestive heart failure. With two young children and a beautiful wife I knew I had to make some lifestyle changes. This began my low sodium journey. I invite you to come to this page and see how it grows as my journey continues. Thanks and feel free to reach out with your own low sodium product finds, heart health journey, or just to say hi
